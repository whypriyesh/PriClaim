Frequently Asked Questions

Is PriClaim HIPAA Compliant? expand_more

Yes, absolutely. PriClaim is SOC2 Type II certified and fully HIPAA compliant. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit, ensuring maximum security for PHI.

Does it integrate with Epic or Cerner? expand_more

Yes, our Pro and Enterprise plans offer seamless API integration with major EHR platforms including Epic, Cerner, Allscripts, and MEDITECH.

How accurate is the AI audit? expand_more

Our AI models are trained on millions of historical claims and achieve a 99.9% accuracy rate in flagging potential policy violations and coding errors.

Can I customize the audit rules? expand_more

Yes, Pro and Enterprise users can define custom rule sets specific to their hospital's internal guidelines or specific payer contracts.
